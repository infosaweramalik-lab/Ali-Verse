🔥 Free Fire Guide

What is Free Fire?

Free Fire is one of the most popular battle royale games. Players land on an island, collect weapons, survive against enemies, and try to become the last player alive.

Top 5 Beginner Tips

  • Land in safe places.
  • Collect weapons quickly.
  • Always keep medkits.
  • Stay inside the safe zone.
  • Practice aiming every day.

Best Weapons

  • MP40
  • M1887
  • AK47
  • Groza
  • AWM

How to Win More Matches

Use cover during fights, improve your aim, and play with a good strategy instead of rushing every enemy. Learning the map and managing your resources can greatly improve your chances of getting a Booyah.
