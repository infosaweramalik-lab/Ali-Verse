🎯 PUBG Mobile Guide

What is PUBG Mobile?

PUBG Mobile is one of the most popular battle royale games. Players land on an island, collect weapons, survive against enemies, and become the last player standing.

Top 5 Beginner Tips

  • Land in safe locations.
  • Collect weapons quickly.
  • Always carry medkits.
  • Stay inside the safe zone.
  • Practice aiming every day.

Best Weapons

  • M416
  • AKM
  • UMP45
  • AWM
  • DP-28
